About Us

Pick-A-Book bookstore was founded by Mr Yamaichi and Mr Sam Lee on the 1st January 2020. The aim of Pick-A-Book bookstore is to make more varieties of books available to the locals and also in the same time to incalcate good reading habits among the population. Pick-A-Book currently offers a vast genre of books ranging from Thriller, Children, Fiction, Nonfiction and Cookbooks, just to name few. The bookstore is opened 7 days a week (10.00am to 10.00pm) to satisfy the reading and book shopping needs for all of our customer. So, what are still waiting for? Do browse through our exciting catalogues of books or drop by our bookstore yourself to pick up an exciting book of your choice today.
