At Pick-A-Book bookstore, we aim to provide you with the following services to serve your book-buying needs:

Special Book Order:

If you would like to purchase a book that we do not carry in our bookstore, kindly reach out to us via email at customerservice@pickabook.store, give us a call at (65) 9123 3219 (Mon – Fri, excluding public holidays: 10.00am to 7.00pm) or walk in to our bookstore and approach any of our staff for assistance.

Customer Enquiries:

Please do reach out to us at (65) 9123 3219 between 10.00am to 7.00pm (Monday to Friday, excluding public holidays) should you have any enquiries.

Reservation Service:

To make a reservation for a book, you may approach us via one of the two following ways as listed below:

  • Call us at (65) 9123 3219 between 10.00am to 7.00pm (Monday to Friday, excluding public holidays)
  • Walk in to our bookstore any time between 10.00am to 10.00pm
Please note that we can only reserve a book for you up to 5 days.

Delivery:

Free delivery within mainland Singapore (excluding offshore islands, Jurong Island, Sentosa) for orders with nett purchase of S$100 and above in a single receipt. Do reach out to our Customer Service at (65) 9123 3219 (Mon – Fri, excluding public holidays: 10.00am to 7.00pm) for enquiries on delivery charge.

Payment Mode:

Pick-A-Book bookstore accept payment in the form of cash, nets, or via GrabPay, MasterCard, Visa or American Express

Exchange Policy:

  • Exchanges will be accepted within 7 days of purchase. Please ensure the book is in a good and saleable before bringing in the book for exchange. Do bring along the original purchase receipt when visiting the store.
  • We regret that no refund will be made.
